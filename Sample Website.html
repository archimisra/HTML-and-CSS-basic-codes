about the bookworms shop

since 1933, the bookworm bookshop has offered rare and hard to find titles for the discerning readers.
The bookworm offers:

  • Friendly, Knowledgeable, and courteous help
  • Free coffee and juice for our customers
  • A well-lit reading room so you can "try- before-you-buy"
  • four friendly cats: emeralds, Catherine, leo, mark

    • our hours are 10 am to 9pm weekdays, noon to 7 on weekends.
